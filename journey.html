Milestones & Visits

From our very first school visit to our most recent workshop — every step of our mission, recorded here.

🗓 January 2025 · Phnom Penh

Insulight is Founded

A small group of passionate students came together with one idea: go to schools and talk about diabetes. Insulight Cambodia was born.

🌱 Founding
🗓 February 2025 · Phnom Penh

First School Visit

We conducted our pilot workshop at a local high school. Over 80 students participated in an interactive session on blood sugar, symptoms, and prevention. The response was overwhelming.

🏫 School Visit #1
🗓 March 2025 · Phnom Penh

Khmer Resource Kit Launched

We designed and printed our first set of Khmer-language educational materials — simple, visual guides on diabetes prevention that students could take home.

📋 Materials
🗓 April 2025 · Kandal Province

Expanding Beyond Phnom Penh

Our first out-of-city visit brought us to two schools in Kandal Province, reaching over 150 rural students who had never received health education of this kind.

🏫 School Visit #3 & #4
🗓 May 2025

Website Launched

Insulight went digital! Our new website helps us share our story, recruit volunteers, and accept donations to fuel future school visits.
