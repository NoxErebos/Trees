What We Do

How We Fight Diabetes

Our multi-step approach ensures students don't just hear about diabetes — they understand it, recognize it, and know how to prevent it.

Why it matters in Cambodia

Diabetes affects over 700,000 adults in Cambodia, yet awareness remains critically low. Early education is the most powerful tool we have to reverse this trend.

Learn More

School Outreach

We travel to schools in urban and rural areas, delivering engaging, age-appropriate workshops on diabetes, blood sugar, and healthy nutrition.

Know the Signs

Students learn the early warning signs of Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes — knowledge that could help them identify risks in themselves and their families.

Prevention First

We teach practical, affordable lifestyle choices — from diet and exercise to stress management — that reduce diabetes risk for life.

Resource Kits

Every school receives printed materials in Khmer, so students and teachers can revisit and share what they've learned long after our visit.
