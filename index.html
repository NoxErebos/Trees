What We Do

How We Document Trees

Our multi-step process identifies, documents, and catalogs each tree.

Campus Exploration

Our exploration team systematically surveys the JPA campus to identify and document each tree.

Research

Our research team gathers information about each tree, and writes a short description.

Documentation

Our documentation team creates a detailed poster for all recorded trees, while our web developers upload the data directly to our website.
