Who We Are

A Community United Against Diabetes

Insulight Cambodia is a youth-led, volunteer-driven organization bringing diabetes education to schools across the Kingdom of Cambodia.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

We believe that every student in Cambodia deserves to understand the risks of diabetes and how to live a life that prevents it. Our mission is to deliver that education — freely, accessibly, and memorably.

  • Our Mission Go to schools and teach students about diabetes — its causes, symptoms, and how to prevent it through healthy habits.
  • Our Vision A Cambodia where no one develops diabetes from lack of knowledge — where every young person is empowered to make healthy choices.
  • Our Approach Hands-on workshops, visual materials in Khmer, interactive Q&A sessions, and follow-up resources for teachers and parents.

Why Diabetes?

Cambodia is experiencing a quiet crisis. Type 2 diabetes rates are rising rapidly due to shifting diets, urbanization, and low health literacy. Yet it is largely preventable with the right knowledge.

Curriculum

What We Teach in Schools

Our workshops are designed to be engaging, accurate, and actionable — even for students with no prior health knowledge.

What is Diabetes?

Understanding Type 1 vs Type 2, the role of insulin, and how the body regulates blood sugar — explained simply and clearly.

Risk Factors

Family history, diet, weight, activity levels — students learn what puts them or their loved ones at risk and what they can change.

Spotting the Signs

Thirst, fatigue, blurred vision, slow healing — recognizing symptoms early can make all the difference in getting timely treatment.

Eating for Health

Practical guidance on balanced Cambodian diets, reducing sugar intake, and making affordable healthy choices every day.

Active Lifestyles

Exercise doesn't require a gym. We teach students how everyday movement dramatically reduces their long-term diabetes risk.
