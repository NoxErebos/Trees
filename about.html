Who We Are

A Class Documenting all Trees On The Jay Pritzker Academy Campus

JPA Trees is a youth-led, volunteer-driven organization bringing tree documentation to schools across the Jay Pritzker Academy Campus.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

We believe that every student in Cambodia deserves to understand the risks of diabetes and how to live a life that prevents it. Our mission is to deliver that education — freely, accessibly, and memorably.

  • Our Mission Document and educate others about the different trees on the Jay Pritzker Academy campus.
  • Our Vision JPA students with a deep understanding of the different trees on the campus.
  • Our Approach On-site documentation, online cataloging, poster creation, and guided tours.

Why Document Trees?

People in our current day and age are increasingly disconnected from the natural world around them. Documenting trees helps us understand and appreciate the environment we live in.
